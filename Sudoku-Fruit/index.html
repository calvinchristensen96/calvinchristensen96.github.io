How to play

The goal of Sudoku Fruit is the same as a traditional game of Sudoku, with a small twist. You need to fill out every empty square of the table with a number between 1 and 9, making sure that every number only occurs once in each row, column, and cell.

The twist is that after you enter in a number, the next square you fill will also fill with color (indicated by the "Sodoku Fruit" Logo glowing orange. Every time you change a square, your next action will alternate between being colorful and colorless. After you've completed a cell, the number of colorful squares in it will be entered into the respective square in the extra cell below the table. The extra cell is like any other, you'll want to make sure that each number (1 through 9) only appears once.
