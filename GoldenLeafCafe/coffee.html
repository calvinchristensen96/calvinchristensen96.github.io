The Coffee Bean Story

From berry to coffee bean the economic impact of coffee cannot be overstated. It is one of the most valuable primary products in world trade, in many years second only to oil as a source of foreign exchange to producing countries. Coffee is crucial to the economies and politics of many developing countries ; for many of the world's Least Developed Countries, exports of coffee account for more than 50 percent of their foreign exchange earnings.

-The International Coffee Organization
