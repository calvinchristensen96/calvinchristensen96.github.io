About Us:

The Golden Leaf Cafe is a local Menomonie coffee shop proudly serving Dunn County and neighboring communities of North-Western Wisconsin

We offer handmade sandwiches, homemade soups and salads, breakfast bowls, pastries, scones, pies and cookies. Take a look at our natural and organic based menu. We have many Vegan, Vegetarian and Gluten Free options.

Try our full espresso and tea bar. Our coffee and espresso is made with our unique blend of fresh roasted in house coffee beans. Try in a drink or bring home a bag to drink at home.

We also offer event catering both onsite and delivered to your location. Private conference room is available upon request. Outdoor seating is available when seasonally permitted.

Stop in store to explore our unique collection of teas, soaps and body care products!

Our Mission:

"Golden Leaf Cafe was created to sell unique recipes and products using high quality, natural and organic ingredients. A supportive organization in Menomonie, Wisconsin that is committed to benefiting both local and global communities."
