Acai Bowl

Ingredients

  • 1 frozen acai puree packet
  • 1/2 cup milk or apple juice
  • 1 frozen banana
  • 1/2 cup frozen strawberries
  • 1/2 cup frozen blueberries
  • Additional optional toppings:
    • Kiwi
    • Granola
    • Strawberries
    • Coconut flakes
    • Banana
    • Nuts
    • etc.

Recipe

  1. Combine acai puree, milk or juice, frozen banana, frozen strawberries, and frozen blueberries into blender.
  2. Blend until combined and smooth.
  3. Pour into a bowl and combine with any additional toppings.
