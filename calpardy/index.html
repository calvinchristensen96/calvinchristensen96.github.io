Calpardy!

Calpardy is a trivia game by Calvin Christensen
Questions and answers are supplied from jService, an open source archive of Jeopardy clues.
Quick Play
Jump in and start playing now. Categories are chosen randomly and can be reset. This mode isn't played for score.
Free Play
Search for specific categories and answer questions at your own pace.
Regular Game
Compete against other players locally to see who can earn the most amount of points. Categories and questions are chosen randomly.
Players: - 3 +
Custom Game
Create your own game board with your own questions and answers.

How to play

  1. There are 3 categories with 3 questions each. Click on the squares underneath to select a question.
  2. Answer the question aloud or in your head, then click "Show Answer". If you got it right click "✓", otherwise click "X".
  3. After answering all 9 questions you get 1 bonus attempt for an additional point. Choose the category you are most confident with.
Have fun!
