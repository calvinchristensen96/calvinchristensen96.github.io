What is Overwatch Pokédex?

A personal project I'm working on that combines two of my favorite games: Overwatch™ and Pokémon. Whenever I encounter a username in Overwatch™ that is the same as an existing Pokémon name, I capture a screenshot and upload it here. My goal is to complete the entire Pokédex this way.
