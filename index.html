About Me

Hi! My name is Calvin Christensen. I am a web developer based out of Wisconsin, USA. I graduated from the University of Wisconsin - Eau Claire in December, 2018 with a degree in Computer Science, Mathematics, and Spanish language.

During my time at the university I interned as a web developer. Since graduation, I have volunteered as a WordPress developer, freelanced as a Full-Stack developer and designer, and currently work full-time in IT. Continue reading below for more details on my experience.

Portfolio

Take a look below to view projects that I've completed.

Chordy

Chordy is a web application I developed to help identify musical chords. This chord dictionary includes two pages: one which gives visual representations of how to play the chords on piano, and the other which allows the user to lookup the name of a chord by selecting the notes which are played.

Pokemon Go Combat Point Maximizer

A web application that I created for use with the mobile video-game Pokemon Go, by Niantic. This application provides detailed stats to help the user identify which individual values a selected Pokemon needs to have in order to maximize it's level.

Golden Leaf Cafe

The Golden Leaf Cafe is a coffeeshop in Menomonie, Wisconsin. I designed and developed the front-end and back-end of their commercial website. This site consists 6 pages including an interactive menu and links to an online ordering platform. The website was active from March 2020 until January 2022, and my work is available on my Github.

Cerveza Santa Maria

Cerveza Santa Maria is a brewery in Puebla, Mexico. I helped develop a version of their website during my internship with B!RTH in Puebla in the summer of 2018. The source code of my work is available on my Github.
