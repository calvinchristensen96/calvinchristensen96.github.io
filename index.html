About Me

My name is Calvin Christensen. I am a web developer based out of North-Western Wisconsin, USA. I graduated from the University of Wisconsin - Eau Claire in December, 2018 with a degree in Computer Science, Mathematics, and Spanish language.

During my time at the university I interned as a web developer. Since graduation, I have volunteered as a WordPress developer, freelanced as a Full-Stack developer and designer, and currently work full-time in IT. Continue reading below for more details on my experience.

Portfolio

Take a look below to view projects that I've completed.

Chordie

Chordie is a web application I developed to help identify musical chords. This chord dictionary includes two pages: one gives visual representations of chords on piano, and the other allows the user to lookup chords in reverse by selecting the notes which are played.

Pokemon Go Potential Maximizer

A web application that I created for use with the mobile video-game Pokemon Go, by Niantic. This application provides detailed stats to help the user identify which individual values a selected Pokemon needs to have in order to maximize it's level.

Golden Leaf Cafe

The Golden Leaf Cafe is a coffeeshop in Menomonie, Wisconsin. I designed and developed the front-end and back-end of their commercial website on March 2020. This site consists 6 pages including an interactive menu and links to an online ordering platform.

Cerveza Santa Maria

Cerveza Santa Maria is a brewery in Puebla, Mexico. I helped develop a version of their website during my internship with B!RTH in Puebla in the summer of 2018. The source code for my work is available on my Github.
