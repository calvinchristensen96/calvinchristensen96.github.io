Welcome to Rainbow Fitness

Your colorful journey to a balanced fitness routine

Rainbow Fitness is designed to make workouts more engaging and motivating by associating each type of exercise with a different color of the rainbow. Users can track their progress by aiming to complete a rainbow of activities each week.


• • • • • •


High-Intensity Interval Training (HIIT)
HIIT involves short bursts of intense exercise followed by periods of rest or low-intensity exercise.

These exercises are designed to improve cardiovascular fitness, burn calories, and build strength quickly.

Examples:
  • Burpees
  • Lunges
  • Jumping Jacks
  • Running

Suggested amount: 75 minutes per week.

Cardio
Cardio exercises are designed to increase heart rate and improve the efficiency of the cardiovascular system.

These exercises are typically performed at a moderate intensity for a longer duration.

Examples:
  • Cycling
  • Jogging
  • Rollerblading
  • Running

Suggested amount: 150 minutes per week.
Your goal: minutes per week.
Flexibility & Yoga
Flexibility & Yoga exercises focus on stretching and elongating the muscles to improve range of motion, reduce injury, and promote relaxation.

Examples:
  • Stretching
  • Yoga

Suggested amount: 30 minutes per week.
Your goal: minutes per week.
Nutrition
This category emphasizes maintaining a balanced diet that includes essential nutrients.

A balanced diet focuses on fruits, vegetables, lean proteins, and whole grains.

Examples:
  • Eating 5 servings of fruits and vegetables per day
  • Drinking 8 glasses of water daily
  • Avoiding processed foods and added sugars

Suggested amount: 7 days of reaching your nutrition goals per week.
Your goal: days per week.
Mindfulness
Mindfulness practices are designed to improve mental health, reduce stress and enchance overall well-being through focused attention and awareness.

Examples:
  • Meditation sessions
  • Deep breathing exercises

Suggested amount: 60 minutes per week.
Your goal: minutes per week.
Rest & Recovery
This category focuses on the importance of rest, including adequate sleep and rest days.

Adhering to rest and recovery can allow the body to repair and rejuvenate.

Examples:
  • Getting 7-9 hours of sleep per night
  • Taking 1-2 rest days per week with no intense physical activity
  • Practicing relaxation techniques before bedtime to improve sleep quality

Suggested amount: 7 days of reaching your rest & recovery goals per week.
